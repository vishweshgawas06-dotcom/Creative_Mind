FAQ

Common questions

Do I need to pay the full amount upfront? ?

No. I work with a 50% upfront payment and 50% on delivery. This protects both of us and keeps things fair.

Can I request changes after delivery? ?

Yes! All packages include free revisions during development. After delivery, you get free support (15–45 days depending on plan) for any issues.

How do we communicate during the project? ?

Primarily via WhatsApp for quick updates and email for documents. I provide daily progress updates so you're never in the dark.

Will I own the code after delivery? ?

100% yes. Once the project is paid for, all code belongs to you. I'll also hand over the full source code and deployment instructions.

Do you offer hosting? ?

I don't provide hosting myself, but I'll help you set up on affordable platforms like Railway, Render, or a VPS. I handle the full deployment for you.

What if my project is more complex? ?

No problem. The Premium plan is a starting price. After a free consultation call, I'll give you a custom quote based on your exact requirements.
